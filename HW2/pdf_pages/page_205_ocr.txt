Y     Inference Approaches

Best-of-N search generates N complete responses and selects the best one among them.
Sentence-level Beam Search generates multiple candidate options for each sentence and chooses the best one.

Stage-level Beam Search generates candidates for each reasoning stage (e.g., summary, caption, reasoning, and
conclusion) and selects the best option at each stage.
