Question: Which of these cities is marked on the map?
Context: N/A
Options:        (A) Las Vegas              (B) San Francisco               (C) Phoenix                (D) Salt Lake City

Rationale : The city is Las Vegas, Nevada. Salt Lake City, Phoenix, and San Francisco are marked with gray circles on
the map below.
Answer: The answer is (A).
